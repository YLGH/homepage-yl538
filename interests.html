Table Tennis


I've been playing table tennis for about four years now. My clubs include the Austin Table Tennis Club, Cambridge University Table Tennis Squad, and (hopefully) MIT's Table Tennis Team. I play mainly recreationally, but have had professional coaching (including one in China) before. I use a Timo Boll carbon spirit blade with DHS Hurricane 8 on the forehand and either tenergy or short pimples on my backhand. My favorite players are Ma Long and Ryu-seung Min.
